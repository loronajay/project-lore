# LORE

## About

In a dying exile village on the edge of a medieval kingdom, sixteen-year-old Leo is trying to survive the slow collapse of the only world he has ever known. Haunted by the loss of his parents, hounded by a brutal local rival, and surrounded by whispers of a curse that is driving villagers into sickness and violence, Leo lives at the bottom of a world built to forget people like him. When a string of terrifying events tears open the fragile routine of his life, he is pulled toward buried truths his kingdom was never meant to reveal.

As Leo begins to uncover the shadow left behind by his father, he is drawn into a much larger struggle shaped by stolen history, manipulated religion, and a prophecy twisted by those in power. What begins as a story of hunger, grief, and survival grows into a grounded fantasy adventure about inheritance, identity, and the cost of truth in a kingdom built on lies.
