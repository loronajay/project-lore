# Act 1 Film Outline

This is the production-facing outline for the active early film material.

## Current adaptation target

Adapt the current Act 1 material up through Leo being caught inside Richard's hidden workspace.

## Sequence blocks

### Sequence A: dream and deprivation

Purpose:
- establish dread
- establish Leo's hunger and loneliness
- establish his mother and father as emotional absence

Core beats:
- battlefield dream
- waking in the bare house
- jewelry box hesitation
- failed attempt to sell the dish

### Sequence B: Richard and the bread market

Purpose:
- introduce Richard as an unreadable force
- show Leo's dependence without making him passive
- establish warm family contrast through memory

Core beats:
- Richard buys the dish
- Leo gets bread money
- market waiting
- warm family memory

### Sequence C: sickness rupture

Purpose:
- reveal the village's social fear
- make the sickness physical and frightening
- push Leo into deeper instability

Core beats:
- public attack
- crowd panic
- Leo nearly gets hit
- bread lost
- nighttime hunger and collapse
- crystal dream

### Sequence D: debt, chase, and humiliation

Purpose:
- bind Leo's need, shame, and fear together
- establish Marshe as personal pressure

Core beats:
- bread appears on the dish
- Leo decides to find Richard
- Marshe notices him
- chase
- beating

### Sequence E: aftermath and memory pressure

Purpose:
- deepen Leo's relationship to his father
- show failed imitation
- show village harassment

Core beats:
- return home
- memory of Jack versus Marcus
- boys throw rocks
- Leo attempts to train
- memory of Jack's fluid combat style
- Leo refuses to enter father's room

### Sequence F: loss, recovery, and forbidden discovery

Purpose:
- push Leo into emotional bottoming-out
- lead him to Richard's hidden truth

Core beats:
- Leo realizes the jewelry box is missing
- he retraces his path
- he avoids the alley, then forces himself back
- he finds the box but the jewelry is gone
- he takes a different route home
- he senses heat by the house
- he sees the unfinished sword
- he slips inside to hide from Marshe
- Richard catches him
