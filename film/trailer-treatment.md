# LORE Trailer Treatment

This version is written as a novelized trailer blurb: something closer to an "about the movie" section than a scene-by-scene trailer breakdown.

## Novelized Trailer Blurb

In a dying exile village at the edge of a medieval kingdom, sixteen-year-old Leo has grown up beneath the weight of hunger, grief, and a silence no one around him seems willing to break. His father is gone. His mother survives only in fragments of warmth and memory. Around him, the village is shrinking under a sickness people call a curse, and fear has become so ordinary that it passes for daily life.

Leo has spent his whole life trying to survive a world that was designed to forget people like him. But when strange dreams begin to press against the edges of his reality, and the shadows around his family start to feel less like tragedy and more like concealment, he is drawn toward a truth buried far beyond the walls of his home. What begins in one collapsing village soon opens into a far larger world: walled cities, royal courts, forbidden knowledge, ancient signs, and a kingdom whose glory has been built over erased blood and rewritten memory.

As violence closes in and hidden loyalties begin to surface, Leo finds himself pulled out of the only life he has ever known and into an unfolding journey far bigger than survival. Beyond the village lies a world of soldiers, castles, secrets, and prophecy, where every answer leads deeper into danger and every step forward reveals that the story of his family may be tied to the fate of the kingdom itself. Somewhere ahead, kings guard their lies, the wrong heirs wear crowns, and the past waits like a door that was never meant to be opened.

`LORE` is a grounded fantasy adventure about inherited grief, stolen history, hidden power, and the moment a forgotten boy is pulled toward the vast and dangerous world that was always waiting beyond the edge of his sorrow.

## Short Version

In a cursed exile village on the edge of a medieval kingdom, a grieving boy is pulled into a dangerous adventure that leads from buried family secrets to the hidden truth behind the kingdom itself.

## Tone Notes

This version is meant to feel:
- atmospheric
- mythic without over-explaining
- emotionally intimate
- adventure-forward beyond the opening setup
- restrained about plot specifics
- suitable for an about page, pitch page, or trailer description
