## What we are locking in this session

### Lock now

- face identity
- hair shape
- age read
- emotional baseline
- clothing silhouette direction
- poverty level

### Do not try to lock now

- injured variant
- warm-memory variant
- fight/training pose
- dream-specific styling
- final lighting system

## Generation target A: portrait

### Goal

Find the best face-forward version of present-day Act 1 Leo.

### Needed read

- about 16
- same underlying identity as the teen concept art
- emotionally vulnerable
- tired
- observant
- poor, not stylish
- grounded fantasy, not heroic fantasy

### Good portrait setup

Prefer:
- chest-up or shoulders-up
- neutral or lightly textured background
- soft but naturalistic light
- worn clothing visible at collar/shoulders
- expression that suggests pressure, grief, or hunger

Avoid:
- weapons
- action pose
- dramatic battle stance
- highly glamorous lighting
- heavy hood shadow hiding the face

## Generation target B: full body

### Goal

Lock Leo's early Act 1 clothing silhouette and material condition.

### Needed read

- poor village boy
- practical, worn clothing
- same face and hair identity as portrait target
- no over-designed adventurer gear
- no noble styling

### Good full-body setup

Prefer:
- standing pose
- relaxed or slightly guarded posture
- visible rough layers
- worn cloth and simple practical wrappings
- neutral background or simple environment

Avoid:
- swords
- daggers
- multiple belts for style
- hood-as-main-identity
- assassin/rogue silhouette

## Suggested prompt direction

Use this as a starting point, then adjust to fit the tool:

`grounded fantasy teenage boy, about 16, same core look as a dark-haired village teen, messy dark hair, expressive tired eyes, slim from deprivation, grieving and observant, poor exile-village clothing, worn rough-spun fabric, muted earth tones, practical simple layers, vulnerable but not fragile, cinematic realism, tactile textures, natural light`

## Optional reinforcing language

Add only if needed:
- quiet sadness
- socially isolated village boy
- weathered cloth
- understated medieval poverty
- intimate portrait

## Negative direction

Actively push away from:
- fantasy hero
- prince
- assassin
- rogue
- battle-ready
- elite swordsman
- ornate costume
- polished leather gear
- anime hero energy

## Approval rubric

Approve an image if it satisfies most of the following:

| Check | Pass if true |
| --- | --- |
| Identity | Feels like the same person as the teen concept-art Leo |
| Age | Reads around 16 |
| Vulnerability | Emotional openness or pressure is visible |
| Poverty | Clothing feels poor and lived-in |
| Non-heroic read | Does not look like a ready-made fantasy protagonist |
| Specificity | Feels like Leo, not a generic medieval boy |
| Reusability | Could anchor multiple future shots |

## Reject triggers

Reject or downgrade if:
- he looks too old
- he looks too pretty in a glossy or model-like way
- he looks too cool
- he looks too capable in a combat-ready way
- the clothing reads as character-design fashion instead of circumstance
- the face drifts too far from the existing teen Leo identity

## Batch strategy

For the first pass:
- generate a small portrait batch
- choose the strongest face identity
- then generate a small full-body batch using that face direction

Do not generate dozens of variations before deciding what the face is.
