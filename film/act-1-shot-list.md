# Act 1 Shot List

This is the first-pass shot plan for the current Act 1 adaptation target.

It is intentionally lean and should be refined after styleframes are approved.

## Shot strategy

- Keep most shots short and purposeful.
- Reserve animation-heavy work for reveals, dream fragments, violence, and emotional turns.
- Let stillness do some of the work.
- Reuse framing patterns and locations to keep production realistic.

## Sequence 1: Dream and deprivation

### Must-have shots

1. Extreme-fragment battlefield images: steel, mud, blood, partial armor, motion blur
2. Leo jolting awake in bed, breath ragged
3. Tight interior details showing the stripped house
4. Leo opening the jewelry box
5. Warm mother memory fragment tied to the box
6. Leo closing the box and taking the dish instead
7. Exterior walking shot placing Leo in the village

### Can stay mostly still

- jewelry box close-up
- dish in Leo's hands
- empty shelf and room details

### Benefits from motion

- dream fragments
- wake-up breath and body jolt
- mother-memory warmth pass

## Sequence 2: Failed market and Richard's first intervention
